About Us.

Exquisite Luxury Events is an arm of The Jameslully Company and has been in the Event and Corporate planning industry since April 12th, 2012. Our services are highly professional but completely bespoke.

We are passionate about using our experience to deliver your dream event. With us, Your event is tailored around our clients and we work in whatever way suits you and your branding style. We plan elegant, stylish, exquisite and enjoyable events.

We will ensure we offer venues and suppliers that reflect the type of events you require.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to craft unforgettable experiences through expertly coordinated events that reflect our clients' visions and surpass their expectations. We are committed to providing remarkable service, meticulous attention to detail, and inventive solutions, ensuring that every event is a resounding success.

OUR OBJECTIVES:

  • Client-Focused Strategy: Recognize and prioritize the distinct requirements and preferences of each client in order to customize every event to align perfectly with their vision and objectives.
  • Outstanding Service: Deliver exceptional customer care throughout the entire planning and execution phases, ensuring transparent communication, prompt responses, and personalized service.
  • Creative Innovations: Keep abreast of market trends while integrating imaginative concepts and advanced technologies to elevate the event experience and present new, exhilarating options.
  • Streamlined Execution: Efficiently coordinate all aspects of event planning to guarantee seamless execution and a flawless outcome that delights clients and attendees alike.

OUR PROCESS

01. CONSULTATION

We begin by understanding your vision through a personalized consultation. During this session, we listen to your ideas, discuss your goals, and identify your preferences. This ensures we’re aligned from the start, crafting a plan that reflects your style and guarantees a seamless, unforgettable event.

Swipe

02. PACKAGE REVIEW

After our consultation, we’ll present a tailored package that meets your specific needs. We’ll walk you through each detail, ensuring everything aligns with your vision and budget. This is your opportunity to review, adjust, and finalize the perfect plan for a flawless event.

Swipe

03. PAYMENT OF NON-REFUNDABLE DEPOSIT

To secure your booking, a non-refundable deposit is required. This ensures your date is reserved and allows us to begin planning your event. The deposit will be applied toward the total event cost, with the remaining balance due as outlined in your agreement.

Swipe

04. A DEDICATED PROJECT MANAGER ASSIGNED

Once booked, a dedicated project manager will be assigned to your event. They will be your main point of contact, overseeing every detail from start to finish. With a focus on clear communication and seamless coordination, they'll ensure your event runs smoothly and exceeds your expectations.

Swipe

05. PLANNING

We handle all the details, turning your vision into a solid plan. From vendor selection to timelines, we manage everything to ensure seamless coordination. Our goal is to make the planning process stress-free while delivering an event that exceeds your expectations.

Swipe

06. EXECUTION

On the day of your event, we handle every detail, ensuring everything runs smoothly. Our team oversees setup, manages vendors, and handles any last-minute adjustments, so you can relax and enjoy a flawless, stress-free event that reflects your vision perfectly.
